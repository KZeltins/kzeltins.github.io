Will agent Jason complete his mission?

Watch the new blockbuster movie "Mission Impossible: C3" to see if agent Jason will successfully crack the arts centre at NYU Abu Dhabi

"Mission Impossible: C3" follows Jason - a superagent from Shanghai. He has been sent on a mission to go to NYU Abu Dhabi (NYU AD), and attend Communications Lab class from the Interactive Media curricula. Although the feat might sound easy at first, don't underestimate the difficulties that NYU AD Arts Center can bring upon lone wonderers. It is up to Jason to navigate the dark alleys of C3 and try to successfully complete his mission - make the class on time.
